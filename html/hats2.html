HATS
Hawai'i Advanced Technology Society

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between Students, Alumni, Professors, Professionals, And Hawaii's Future


We believe that promoting technology to Hawaii's youth through awareness and discussion of technological related issues are the keys to solving the problems of tomorrow.

What we do

We teach. We learn. We promote.

We also enjoy more than a laugh or two whenever it can be spared.

But that doesn't mean we're not serious about what we do.

Our Specializations

Security

There's a potential for flaws in every system. We're concerned with this. We believe in securing our systems and enviroments. We do so through the use of penetration testing, active monitoring, and more importantly - user education.

Software

Without software, our underlying computer infastructure becomes incredibly tedious to utilize. Understanding this piece of the puzzle is key to successfully understanding how everything comes together.

Systems

Our fundamental architecture. These are the devices that make our world run as we know it. They range from simple switches to complex virtualized machines potentially running hundreds of different servers on one device.

Our Targets

Students

Our main target. You're the lifeline of what we have. Without our students we don't have a club. You all are the day to day experience that makes this club worthwhile. We welcome all students to come and join us from any degree program or campus. Regardless of your level of knowledge or experience. We're all here to learn.

Alumni and Industry Professionals

Former students now turned mentor. You're already in the field slaying dragons. You know your way around a router and a PowerEdge server isn't something that you're frightened of anymore. We want to bridge the gap between former and current students by giving them an avenue to discuss technological developments in Hawaii.

Faculty

We see you everyday. We learn from you both technically and personally. You're our true role models at the end of the day and shape the experiences our lives can potentially have. We invite you to come into our realm and see what we're like outside of class. We'll often have student run presentations on projects or ideas they've been working on. Come by and see what the fruits of your labor have sowed.

Hawaii's Youth

You're the future just as much as we are. We want to get you interested as well. We're looking to promote students to get into STEM fields and show them the options they have to stay in Hawaii and get the proper training they need to grow.
